Fabrication

Precision stainless-steel fabrication and welding support for industrial and sanitary applications. Fortis Industrial Solutions provides fabrication and welding support for new equipment, system modifications, repairs, and upgrades across food & beverage, pharmaceutical, biotech, and industrial manufacturing environments. We support projects ranging from small on-site repairs to complete custom fabrication, delivering durable, accurately built components that integrate seamlessly into existing systems. Our work is executed with a strong focus on fit-up, weld quality, finish requirements, and long-term performance.
