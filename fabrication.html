Post-Weld Treatments

Post-weld treatment supports corrosion resistance, removes heat tint/scale, and improves cleanability‚Äîespecially in sanitary environments.

Passivation

  • Restores protective chromium oxide layer
  • Removes free-iron contamination
  • Common references: ASTM A967 / ASTM A380 (as applicable)

Pickling

  • Removes weld scale, oxides, and heat tint
  • Often performed before passivation
  • Useful for heavily oxidized weld zones

Mechanical Weld Cleaning

  • Blending/smoothing and defect removal
  • Prep for finishing or sanitary service
  • Supports consistent appearance

Polishing / Surface Finishing

  • Brushed / polished finishes (per spec)
  • Improves cleanability and hygiene
  • Internal/external touch-ups (scope-dependent)

Post-weld services can be performed in-house for fabricated components or on-site for installed systems (scope dependent).
