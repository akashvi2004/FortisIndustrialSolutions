Cleaning and Passivation

Passivation removes embedded contaminants—such as free iron, weld oxides, and surface particulates—that arise during fabrication, machining, and handling. Through chemical treatment, we restore stainless steel to its original corrosion-resistant state, improving both durability and sanitary performance.

Our Cleaning & Passivation Process

  • Pre-Cleaning: Degreasing and cleaning to remove organic residues and debris.

  • Chemical Passivation:
    • Nitric Acid: Ideal for high-chromium stainless steels (304/316/316L).
    • Citric Acid: Eco-friendly option, enhanced with chelating agents for product purity.
    • Phosphoric Acid: Used selectively for special environments.

  • Pickling (Optional): Restores surface finish after welds and oxidation using blended acids.

Testing & Documentation

  • Copper sulfate test (ASTM A967)
  • Water immersion and rinse evaluations
  • Visual inspections for etching and discoloration
  • Certificate of Compliance with ASTM A380 and A967
