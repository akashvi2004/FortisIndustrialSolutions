Design and Drafting

At Fortis Industrial Solutions, we offer extensive design capabilities that cater to a wide range of industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing. Our team specializes in designing highly functional, custom-built systems and equipment that meet both operational and regulatory requirements. We work closely with clients to develop solutions that maximize efficiency, hygiene, and safety while maintaining cost-effectiveness.

Types of Equipment & Structures We Design

Tanks

We design various types of tanks used across multiple industries, including food-grade, pharmaceutical, and chemical storage tanks. Whether you're looking for custom storage, fermentation, mixing, or processing tanks, we tailor each design to your specific needs, ensuring it meets industry standards for safety, durability, and cleanliness.

  • Custom storage tanks Tailored to your capacity and material handling needs.
  • Food-grade tanks: Designed to meet sanitary requirements and ensure hygiene.
  • Pharmaceutical tanks: Specialized for stringent cleanliness and regulatory compliance.
  • Mixing and processing tanks Tailored for specific processing needs, including heating or cooling capabilities.

Process Equipment

We specialize in designing a wide range of process equipment, including flowboards, pump mounts, skidded systems, and pump carts. These systems are essential for streamlining industrial processes and ensuring smooth, continuous operations across various production lines.

  • Flowboards: Custom-designed for precise flow control and monitoring.
  • Pump mounts: Designed to secure pumps in place for optimal performance and safety.
  • Skidded systems: Fully integrated units that include tanks, piping, and pumps, ready for easy installation.
  • Pump carts:Mobile units designed for portability and ease of use in various applications.

Platforms, Staircases, and Landings (OSHA Compliant)

Our design team can create OSHA-compliant platforms, staircases, and landings tailored to your specific operational needs. These structures ensure employee safety while providing easy access to equipment and machinery. We ensure that each design adheres to relevant safety regulations and standards.

  • Custom Platforms: Designed for easy access and operational efficiency.
  • Staircases and Landings: Safe and durable, with compliance to OSHA standards.
  • Accessibility: Providing easy access to machines, tanks, and other industrial systems.

Plant Processing Piping

We design plant process piping systems that are critical for fluid transportation in various industries. Whether it's a simple pipeline or an intricate network of pipes, we ensure that each system is designed for optimal flow, safety, and compliance with industry standards.

  • Custom Piping Layouts: Tailored to your plant’s operational needs.
  • Sanitary Piping Systems: Specifically designed for hygienic processes in the food and pharmaceutical industries.
  • Material Section:Ensuring durability, corrosion resistance, and compatibility with various chemicals and fluids.

BOMs (Bill of Materials) & Fabrication Drawings

We provide detailed Bills of Materials (BOMs) and comprehensive fabrication drawings to ensure that every component is accurately planned and fabricated. These documents provide all the necessary details for procurement and assembly, ensuring that your project is completed on time and within budget.

  • BOM creation: Accurate lists of materials required for your project.
  • Fabrication drawings: Detailed plans for the manufacturing process.
  • Project clarity: Ensures that all components are sourced and built as per the design specifications.

Other Capabilities for the Sanitary Food Industry

Sanitary Piping Systems

We design sanitary piping systems specifically for the food and beverage industry, where cleanliness and hygiene are paramount. Our sanitary systems are designed to ensure easy cleaning, prevent contamination, and meet regulatory standards like FDA, USDA, and 3-A Sanitary Standards.

  • Hygienic Design:Smooth welds and specialized materials for easy cleaning.
  • Compliance: Adherence to food safety standards (FDA, USDA, 3-A).
  • Sanitary Fittings: Tri-clamp, butt-weld, and other food-grade fittings for easy connection and maintenance.

Mixing and Blending

We design mixing and blending systems for food-grade applications, ensuring uniformity and consistency in the production process. These systems are ideal for blending ingredients, emulsifying liquids, and creating consistent food products.

  • Precision blending: Ensures even mixing of ingredients
  • Sanitary construction: Materials and finishes that comply with food safety standards.
  • Custom designs: Tailored to meet the specific needs of your process.

Clean-in-Place (CIP) Systems

Our Clean-in-Place (CIP) systems are designed to automate the cleaning of tanks, pipes, and equipment in food and beverage processing plants. These systems help reduce downtime, increase operational efficiency, and ensure a high standard of cleanliness in your facility.

  • Automated cleaning: Reduces manual labor and improves consistency.
  • Chemical and thermal cleaning: Tailored to your facility’s needs.
  • Reduced downtime: Keeps your equipment clean and ready for production with minimal interruption.

Filtration and Separation Systems

We design filtration and separation systems to ensure that only the purest ingredients or chemicals are processed in your facility. These systems are essential for removing unwanted particles, solids, or contaminants from liquids and gases in the food and pharmaceutical industries.

  • Filtration solutions: Custom-designed for your specific filtration needs.
  • Separation systems: Efficiently separate solids from liquids or gases.
  • Compliance: Adheres to industry standards for food safety and product purity

Additional Services

  • 3D Modeling and Simulation: We provide 3D models and simulations to visualize your designs and test them before physical fabrication.
  • Prototyping: Rapid prototyping services to create mock-ups and ensure the design meets your needs.
  • Maintenance & Upgrades: Ongoing support for system maintenance, improvements, and retrofits to keep your operations running smoothly.
