Design

We provide CAD-driven design for sanitary and industrial systems. From concept to fabrication drawings, our team helps you de-risk builds and meet compliance.

Capabilities

  • 3D modeling & fabrication drawings (tanks, skids, process piping)
  • P&IDs, GA layouts, isometrics, and bill of materials
  • Material selection (304, 316/316L, specialty alloys) & finish specs
  • Sanitary design: drainability, cleanability, and dead-leg elimination
  • Compliance awareness: ASME, CRN, TSSA; ASTM A967/A380 for passivation

Deliverables

  • Fabrication-ready drawings and cut lists
  • Weld maps & QA documentation
  • Installation & commissioning support

Typical Use Cases

  • New process skids & tank systems
  • Upgrades to meet hygienic standards
  • Scale-up of pilot equipment to production
