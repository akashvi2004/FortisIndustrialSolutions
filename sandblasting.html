Sandblasting & Glass Beading Services

Efficient surface cleaning and preparation for stainless steel and metal components.

Sandblasting and glass beading are proven surface preparation techniques used to clean, smooth, and condition metal surfaces prior to further processing. At Fortis Industrial Solutions, we provide both shop-based and mobile sandblasting / glass beading services, enabling efficient preparation of equipment regardless of size or location.

These processes remove rust, scale, contaminants, and old coatings, leaving a clean, uniform surface ready for passivation, coating, painting, or finishing.

Key Benefits

Rust & Scale Removal

Eliminates rust, scale, corrosion products, and surface contamination to restore metal integrity and prevent future degradation.

Smooth & Uniform Finish

Glass beading produces a consistent surface finish that enhances appearance while reducing debris buildup and surface irregularities.

Optimized Surface Preparation

Creates an ideal surface profile for passivation, coatings, or paint adhesion—improving durability and finish life.

Lower Cost & Time

Fast, efficient cleaning reduces rework, minimizes downtime, and lowers overall production and maintenance costs.

Multiple Metal Compatibility

Suitable for stainless steel, carbon steel, and aluminum across a wide range of industrial applications.

Mobile & Shop-Based Capability Our mobile sandblasting and glass beading services allow on-site surface preparation for large or fixed equipment, reducing transportation needs while maintaining consistent finish quality.
