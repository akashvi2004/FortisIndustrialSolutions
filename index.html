About Fortis Industrial Solutions

Precision stainless-steel fabrication, surface finishing, and industrial support for regulated and sanitary process environments.

Fortis Industrial Solutions provides precision stainless-steel fabrication, surface finishing, and industrial support services for highly regulated and sanitary process environments. From custom tanks and skidded systems to on-site passivation and finishing, we support manufacturers and processors in pharmaceutical, food & beverage, biotech, chemical, and industrial sectors with solutions built to specification and delivered with reliability.

Our focus is on engineered outcomes that meet both functional and regulatory demands—helping clients reduce downtime, improve performance, and ensure traceability and compliance through every stage of fabrication and treatment.
