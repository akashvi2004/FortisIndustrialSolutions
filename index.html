Materials We Deal With

  • 304Stainless Steel
    The most widely used stainless steel, offering excellent corrosion resistance, strength, and formability. Ideal for tanks, piping, and general process equipment.
  • 316, 316L Stainless Steel
    Known for superior resistance to chemicals, chlorides, and acidic environments. The low-carbon version (316L) minimizes carbide precipitation, making it perfect for welded assemblies and sanitary applications.
  • Specialty Alloys
    For demanding projects that require enhanced strength, heat resistance, or corrosion protection beyond standard grades. We source and fabricate with alloys tailored to your process needs.
  • Food-Grade and Pharmaceutical-Grade Materials
    All materials are carefully selected and finished to meet strict hygiene, safety, and regulatory requirements, ensuring compliance with FDA, USDA, and GMP standards.
