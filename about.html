Who We Are

Fortis Industrial Solutions is a stainless-steel fabrication and industrial services company supporting sanitary and regulated process environments. We work with manufacturers across food & beverage, dairy, pharmaceutical, biotechnology, chemical, and industrial sectors.

Industries We Serve

  • Food & Beverage Processing
  • Dairy & Cheese Manufacturing
  • Pharmaceutical & Biotechnology
  • Chemical & Industrial Manufacturing
  • Sanitary & Clean-Process Facilities

Experience

Fortis Industrial Solutions provides multi-disciplinary stainless-steel fabrication and industrial services for highly regulated process environments.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end support for stainless-steel equipment and systems—from design and fabrication to surface treatment and on-site services.

Capabilities

  • Custom stainless-steel tank and skid fabrication
  • CAD-driven design and engineering support
  • High-purity welding (TIG and orbital)
  • Electropolishing, mechanical polishing, and surface preparation
  • In-house and on-site stainless-steel passivation services
  • Regulatory documentation and quality compliance (ASME, CRN, TSSA)

Our Approach

We take an engineering-driven and practical approach to every project, with careful consideration for constructability, hygiene, and compliance.

Quality & Compliance

  • ASME-based design and fabrication practices
  • CRN and TSSA documentation support
  • ASTM-compliant surface treatment procedures
  • Inspection records and QA/QC documentation available
